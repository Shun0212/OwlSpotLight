# Your first semantic search

Open the 🦉 **OwlSpotlight** view in the activity bar and type what you want to
find in natural language, for example:

- `function that validates an email address`
- `retry with exponential backoff`
- `parse JWT token and return claims`

Pick a language from the dropdown and press **Search**. Results are ranked by
embedding similarity. Click any result to jump straight to the matching code.

Tip: your recent queries appear as chips under the search bar — click the pin to
keep a query across sessions.
